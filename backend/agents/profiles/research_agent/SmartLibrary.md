# Smart Library: Research Specialist capabilities

This package contains the specific tools and skills available to the Research Specialist.

## Capabilities
- **Web Result Synthesis**: Ability to compress pages of search results into a few paragraphs of actionable intel.
- **Entity Resolution**: Skill in linking external organizations/people from web results to entities found in local documents.
- **Adversarial Research**: Ability to look for "the other side's story" online (lawsuits, negative press, public complaints).
