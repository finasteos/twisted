# Smart Library: Legal Advisor capabilities

This package contains the specific tools and skills available to the Legal Advisor.

## Capabilities
- **Threat Vector Analysis**: Ability to spot legal, financial, and reputational threats in a given scenario.
- **Contractual Review**: Skill in assessing obligations and loopholes in informal and formal agreements.
- **Defensive Positioning**: Skill in formulating a narrative that minimizes client liability.
