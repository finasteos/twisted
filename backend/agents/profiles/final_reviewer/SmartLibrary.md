# Smart Library: Final Reviewer capabilities

This package contains the specific tools and skills available to the Final Reviewer.

## Capabilities
- **Red Teaming**: Ability to aggressively attack a proposed strategy to find its weak points.
- **Synthesis QA**: Skill in checking cross-agent consistency (e.g., does the Strategist's plan violate the Legal Advisor's warning?).
- **Confidence Scoring**: Ability to assign objective success probabilities based on historical patterns and evidence strength.
